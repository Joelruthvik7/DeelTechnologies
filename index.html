Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide talent?
We typically present shortlisted candidates within 48-72 hours of requirement intake.
Do you provide contract-to-hire options?
Yes, we offer flexible contract-to-hire models to ensure cultural and technical fit before permanent hiring.
What industries do you specialize in?
We serve FinTech, Healthcare, E-commerce, Logistics, and High-Tech Enterprise sectors.
What is the bench sales process at Deel?
Our dedicated bench sales team markets your consultants to a vast network of tier-1 vendors and direct clients, ensuring high utilization and billable rates.
Do you handle international placements?
Yes, we have a global delivery model that supports clients in the US, Canada, UK, and India.
How do you verify technical skills?
Every candidate undergoes a multi-stage vetting process including live coding assessments, system design interviews, and behavioral screenings conducted by subject matter experts.
What work authorizations do you support?
We work with US Citizens, Green Card holders, H1B, OPT/EAD, and other valid work authorizations.
Can you scale a team of 10+ people?
Absolutely. We specialize in workforce scalability and can assemble dedicated project teams within weeks for large-scale enterprise initiatives.
What is your screening success rate?
Our client-interview-to-hire ratio is 3:1, significantly higher than the industry average of 8:1, thanks to our rigorous internal vetting.
Do you offer 24/7 support?
Yes, our global operations in multiple time zones ensure that someone is always available to support our clients and consultants.
How do you handle payroll and compliance?
Deel Technologies manages all aspects of payroll, tax compliance, and background checks, removing the administrative burden from our clients.
Can I submit my resume without a specific job?
Yes, we encourage talented IT professionals to submit their resumes to our general database for future high-priority roles.
What are your typical project durations?
Contract roles usually range from 6 to 18 months, with many projects being extended based on performance and budget.
Do you support remote-first companies?
Yes, we have a massive pool of remote-ready talent and provide guidance on remote onboarding and management.
What is the cost for hiring through Deel?
Our pricing models vary by engagement type (Contract, Direct Hire, Project-based). Please contact our sales team for a custom quote.
